# Editing Your Website — A Quick Guide

Your site has a simple online editor. You fill in a form, click **Save**, and the
website updates itself a minute or two later. You never touch any code, and you
can't break the design — the editor only lets you change words, numbers, and links.

---

## Getting in

1. Go to **https://app.pagescms.org**
2. Click **Sign in with GitHub** and log in with your GitHub account.
   *(One-time: if it's your first time, accept the access request for the
   `nos-computer` site.)*
3. Click the **nos-computer** project. You'll see a list of screens on the left.

> If the screen looks empty or says "no configuration," make sure the branch is
> set to **`master`** (there's a small branch menu near the top).

---

## The screens

They're ordered with the things you'll change most often at the top:

- **⭐ Prices** — every price on the site (Service, Virus Removal, OS Install, etc.).
- **⭐ Business Info** — phone, email, address, store hours, Facebook/YouTube links.
- **Services** — the wording on the three service cards and their pop-ups.
- **PC Builds** — the four build tiers and their specs.
- **Hero & Headlines** — the big headline and intro at the top of the page.
- **Warranty** — the warranty time windows and terms.
- **Site & SEO** — page title, the YouTube video, search/social text.

---

## Making a change

1. Click a screen on the left (e.g. **⭐ Prices**).
2. Edit the field you want — just type the new value.
3. Click **Save** (top of the screen).
4. Wait ~1–2 minutes, then refresh your live site:
   **https://yuenvision.github.io/nos-computer/**

That's it. Change the phone number once in **Business Info** and it updates
everywhere on the site automatically — header, footer, contact section, all of it.

### A couple of tips
- For a **price**, the "Shows under which service" dropdown decides which pop-up it
  appears in — leave it as-is unless you're adding a brand-new price line.
- A field labeled "do not change" or "managed by developer" (like internal IDs or
  image files) is best left alone — changing it can hide content.

---

## What you can and can't change here

**You can edit:** all prices, hours, contact info, social links, and the wording
throughout the site (services, builds, hero, warranty), plus the YouTube video.

**Handled by Paul (not in this editor):** the layout and colors, the photo gallery,
the service/build card photos, and the background video at the top. Want any of those
changed? Just text Paul.

---

## Stuck?

Text Paul. Worst case, nothing you do in the editor can take the site down — if a
save ever doesn't look right, the previous version stays live until it's sorted out.
